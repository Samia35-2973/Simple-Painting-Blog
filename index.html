Projects

School Project

In my 1st semester of University, I was assigned to make some posters for children. It was a Cleanliness Project. Unfortunately due to COVID-19, we couldn't continue the project. The first poster contains some cartoons and the other one highlights the importance of keeping our environment clean.

Independence Day Project

Sometimes I help my father with his works. This time he told me to draw something different so that he can submit it to the press conference. It was for his magazine work about Independence Day 1971. The first sketch highlights the Movement. After that I submitted another drawing which was drawn by pastel colours. It took me 2 days to think of an unique concept and 5 days to draw on the paper. I failed to draw my concept a lot of times. Then, after so much efforts, the drawing got selected for the magazine.
